# AEGIS OS Manifesto

Version 0.2.0-alpha

---

# Why AEGIS Exists

Technology should continue serving people when the Internet disappears.

Knowledge should not require subscriptions.

Preparedness should not require expensive equipment.

Communities should not depend entirely on centralized infrastructure.

AEGIS exists to change that.

---

# What We Believe

Knowledge is resilience.

Preparation reduces fear.

Simple systems survive.

Communities are stronger together.

Open systems outlast proprietary ones.

Good engineering is invisible.

The operator should focus on the mission—not the software.

---

# We Reject

Cloud dependence.

Needless complexity.

Vendor lock-in.

Dark patterns.

Telemetry by default.

Interfaces that require training.

Software that fails when disconnected.

---

# We Build

Offline-first tools.

Readable software.

Human-readable data.

Long-lived systems.

Modular architecture.

Portable knowledge.

Community resilience.

---

# Every Contribution Should

Improve usability.

Improve maintainability.

Improve resilience.

Preserve simplicity.

Respect the operator.

Leave the system better than you found it.

---

# Our Promise

AEGIS will remain:

Offline First.

Community Driven.

Open Source.

Human Focused.

Built to Last.

---
