# Emergency Supply Kit

Keep supplies in a place that can be reached quickly. Build the kit around the people, pets, equipment, climate, and medical needs in the household or team.

## Core Supplies

- Water for drinking and sanitation.
- Non-perishable food.
- Battery-powered or hand-crank radio.
- Flashlight or headlamp.
- First aid kit.
- Extra batteries and charging cables.
- Whistle or signal device.
- Dust masks.
- Plastic sheeting and duct tape.
- Moist towelettes, garbage bags, and sanitation supplies.
- Wrench or pliers for utility shutoff when appropriate.
- Manual can opener.
- Local maps.
- Copies of important documents.
- Cash in small bills.

## Field Notes

- Store supplies in durable containers.
- Inspect food, water, batteries, and medicine on a schedule.
- Keep a smaller portable kit ready for evacuation.
- Add assistive devices, glasses, prescriptions, pet supplies, and infant supplies as needed.
