# Hurricane Preparedness

Hurricanes can bring destructive wind, storm surge, inland flooding, tornadoes, and long outages. Prepare before watches or warnings are issued.

## Before The Storm

- Know evacuation zones and routes.
- Make a family or team communication plan.
- Prepare emergency supplies.
- Secure loose outdoor items.
- Protect windows where practical.
- Charge phones, radios, power banks, and medical devices.
- Photograph documents, property, and critical equipment.
- Follow local emergency management instructions.

## During The Storm

- Stay indoors and away from windows.
- Move to an interior room on the lowest safe level.
- Keep emergency communications equipment close.
- Do not walk, swim, or drive through floodwater.
- Use generators outdoors only and away from openings.

## After The Storm

- Avoid downed lines, floodwater, unstable structures, and debris.
- Check on neighbors when conditions are safe.
- Document damage before cleanup.
- Use safe food, water, and generator practices.
- Monitor official local recovery instructions.
