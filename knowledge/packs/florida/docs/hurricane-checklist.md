# Hurricane Checklist

## Before the Storm

- Charge phones, radios, batteries, and power banks.
- Fill water containers.
- Secure outdoor items.
- Test flashlights.
- Review evacuation routes.
- Photograph important documents.
- Freeze water bottles for cooling.

## During the Storm

- Stay indoors.
- Stay away from windows.
- Monitor official alerts.
- Do not run generators indoors or in garages.

## After the Storm

- Avoid flood water.
- Check on neighbors.
- Document damage.
- Use generators outside only.
